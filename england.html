England

About English leagues

England divided their leagues to what they are today till recently in 2004. Before they used to have the Football league First division, and Football league second division. Both of these leagues were around for a long time.

Premier League

The Premier League is the top professional football (soccer) league in England. It is one of the most popular and competitive football leagues in the world. The top four team at the end of the season qualify to play in the Champions League. The Premier League consists of 20 teams, and each team plays a total of 38 matches during a season. Wins are awarded 3 points each, ties are awarded 1 point each, and no points are awarded for losses. The top Team wins the Premier league trophy and the bottom three teams get relegated to the lower league(the EFL Championship). That's why losing is not an option.

EFL Championship

The English Football League (EFL) Championship is the second-highest division in the English football league system, sitting directly below the Premier League. The EFL Championship consists of 24 teams, making it a larger league compared to the Premier League. This league uses the same pointage system as the premier league and the bottom three teams can also be relegated to a lower division, but this time the top three teams of this division will be promoted to the Premier League and have a chance to battle some of the best teams in the world.

League One

EFL League One is the third tier of the English football league system, sitting below the EFL Championship and above EFL League Two. The top two teams in the final league standings are automatically promoted to the Championship. Teams finishing from third to sixth place qualify for the playoffs. This time the bottom four teams will get relegated to a lower league. EFL League One consists of 24 teams. The main objective for teams in League One is to earn promotion to the EFL Championship. The winner of the playoff final gains promotion to the Premier League and is often referred to as the "third promoted team.This division also uses the same pointage system as the EFL Championship and also impliments the same promotion and relegation rules.

League Two

EFL League Two is the fourth and lowest tier of the English football league system, sitting below EFL League One. EFL League Two consists of 24 teams. The primary goal for teams in League Two is to secure promotion to EFL League One. Now the top three teams automatically get promoted to the EFL League One. Teams finishing from fourth to seventh place qualify for the playoffs and the winner will be the forth team moving up to the higher league.

National Team

The English National League is the top tier of non-league football in the English football pyramid. It sits below the English Football League (EFL) and is one level above the regional divisions. They use the same pointage systems as the higher league. This is usually where new teams would start making their proffesional debut and start making themselves known to the world.
