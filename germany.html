Germany

About Germany leagues

German football is split into three main leagues and under those leagues are regional leagues. They all use the same pointage system as all the other leagues that are talked about. Germany is home to some of the most powerful teams in the world.

Bundesliga

The Bundesliga is the top professional football league in Germany, and it is widely regarded as one of the most competitive and well-organized football leagues in the world. The top four teams of this league qualify to play in the Champions League. The Bundesliga consists of 18 teams that compete against each other in a round-robin format. This means that every team goes against every other team twice, one home, and one away. The teams that finish the season in the bottom two positions are relegated to the 2. Bundesliga, the second tier of German football. Teams earn three points for a win, one point for a draw, and no points for a loss. The league table is determined by points, and in case of a tie in points, tiebreakers such as goal difference and goals scored are used to rank teams.

2. Bundesliga

The 2. Bundesliga is the second-tier professional football league in Germany, sitting directly below the Bundesliga. The 2. Bundesliga typically consists of 18 teams that compete against each other in a round-robin format. The top two teams in the 2. Bundesliga standings at the end of the season are directly promoted to the Bundesliga for the following season. The teams that finish the season in the bottom two positions are relegated to the 3. Liga, the third tier of German football.

Liga 3

The 3. Liga is the third-tier professional football league in Germany. It operates below the 2. Bundesliga and above all of the Regional leagues. The 3. Liga usually consists of 20 teams that compete against each other in a round-robin format. The top two teams in the 3. Liga standings at the end of the season are directly promoted to the 2. Bundesliga for the following season. The teams that finish the season in the bottom three positions are relegated to the Regionalliga, the fourth tier of German football.

Regional

This is the Regional league of Germany. This is wear many new teams start their profesional carrer. The top winners of each league after each season will get promoted to the 3.liga division of Germany.
